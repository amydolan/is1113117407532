About Cloud

Software as a Service (SaaS) - Salesforce

According to salesforce.com, Software as a Service is a way of delivering applications over the Internet - as a service. It has a variety of different applications and uses which began primarily as photo sharing and webmail. The growth of SaaS over the past number of years has been phenomenal, similar to the growth of a lot of other technological advances. Salesforce is an example of Software as a Service. Salesforce produces cloud-based applications for sales and marketing among other areas. The attractiveness of Salesforce for firms comes from the idea that the service doesnt require IT experts to set up or manage. Salesforce has also moved with the times and is providing a mobile compatible service and artificial intelligence.

Platform as a Service (PaaS) - Cloud 9

Another form of cloud computing, Platform as a Service provides a medium that allows customers to develop, run and manage applications in a pain and hassle free fashion. An example of this is Cloud 9. Its a cloud-based IDE that permits users to write, run and debug code with just a browser. The facility of a cloud based platform such as Cloud 9 allows users to work on the same project at home, in the office or at school. Cloud 9 makes collaboration easier than ever with the pair-programming feature. Theres also a community space on cloud 9 which allows for further collaboration between users. Cloud 9 supports a number of code repositories including GitHub and deployment to Heroku, Windows Azure and more.

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) - Amazon (AWS)

Techopedia.com sites the definition of IaaS as being a service model that delivers computer infrastructure on an outsourced basis to support enterprise operations. The page furthers this by saying that IaaS typically provides hardware, storage, servers and date center space along with the possibility of software. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) is also known as Hardware as a Service (HaaS). Gartner named Amazon (AWS) as the leader in the IaaS Sector for the 7th Consecutive year last June. Amazon is an example of IaaS as a result of many of its services including Amazon EC2, Amazon Elastic Block Store and Autoscaling. According to Techrepublic.com, AWS has more than double the market share of its next 9 IaaS competitors combined.

Communication as a Service (CaaS) - Gmail

Communications as a service (CaaS) is a collection of different vendor services that facilitate business communications, according to Techopedia.com. Organisations may use these and similar services to lower costs and increase efficiency for business processes involving audio or video telecommunications. Wihtout a doubt, Gmail fits very well into this category. Gmail is used by firms all over the world, along with people using it fo their own personal communication needs and not just for work. Gmail gives an employee the opportunity to communicate with co-workers sitting beside them and co-workers sitting at a desk on the opposite side of the globe. With the option of email, instant messaging and many more, Gmail has certainly made its mark as CaaS.
