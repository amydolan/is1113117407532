What is Cloud Computing?


According to the Microsoft Azure website, cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics and more—over the Internet (“the cloud”). Companies offering these computing services are called cloud providers and typically charge for cloud computing services based on usage, similar to how you are billed for water or electricity at home. The growth of cloud computing in the last number of years has been astronomical and this is due to the huge number of advantages available to those who utilise cloud services to the full potential.

Why the cloud?
